Classes

Beginners

Beginners Course is the very first step towards understanding the theory and practice of yoga for newcomers. It starts with the introduction to the five points of yoga according to Swami Vishnudevananda, followed by explanations and demonstrations of yogic exercises (asanas), deep abdominal breathing teachniques (pranayama), proper relaxation, proper diet, positive thinking and meditation and the 12 basic postures and the surya namaskar (sun salutation). The Beginners Course is an introduction to Yoga and the yogic lifestyle to all the people. Swami Vishnudevananda recommended daily practice of the 12 Basic Asanas. Traditionally, Yogis practice the Surya Namaskar (Sun salutation) before the Asanas. Get ready for the open classes and be able to start your own yoga practice.

MWF : 8AM to 9AM TTHS : 10AM-11AM
